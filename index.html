Flavours We Offer

Who doesn’t love ice cream? it’s one of the first foods I can think of that’s universally loved across all cultures. You can make ice cream out of almost anything so there are literally thousands of flavors from around the world.

Chocolate

Chocolate ice cream is typically made by blending cocoa powder with eggs, cream, sugar, and vanilla. We love the versions made with dark chocolate. The richness and slight bitterness of the dark chocolate is to die for.

Vennila

This is a no-brainer. Vanilla is the most popular ice cream flavor in the world so it only makes sense to start this list with vanilla ice cream. It isn’t the sexiest flavor but it’s definitely one of the most delicious and versatile.Made from vanilla bean, vanilla ice cream is delicious on its own.

Cup IceCreams

Cup ice cream is the best. It’s often the crowning ingredient on halo-halo, a popular Filipino dessert made with shaved ice, condensed milk, and other ingredients like sweetened beans, coconut strips, and sugar palm fruit.
